# Contributing

CERBERUS welcomes technically specific review, especially around causal-model completeness, common-cause failure, FCOI formalization, runtime statistics, switched-system stability, FDIR, and spacecraft operations.

Please distinguish clearly between:

- implemented behavior,
- executable research artifacts,
- specifications,
- proposed mechanisms,
- and validation claims.

A useful contribution states the invariant or claim being challenged, supplies a reproducible counterexample or source where possible, and avoids expanding novelty claims beyond the evidence.
